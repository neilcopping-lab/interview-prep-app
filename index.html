How to answer using STAR

Structure each answer in your head before you speak — it's the difference between rambling and a story the interviewer can follow.

S Situation — set the scene, briefly.
T Task — what you were responsible for.
A Action — what you actually did, step by step.
R Result — the outcome, with a number if you have one.

Tip: say "I," not "we" — the panel is hiring you. And don't rush the Action — it's the part that gets scored.
