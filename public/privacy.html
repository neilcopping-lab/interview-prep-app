Privacy & Data Notice

Draft — last updated 21 July 2026. Review before publishing live.

This notice covers the Interview Prep Report service (the "Service"), operated by The Com'mon People. It explains what personal data we collect when you use the Service, why, how long we keep it, and what rights you have over it, in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

The Com'mon People, contactable at neil.copping@thecommonpeople.co.uk, is the data controller for information collected through this Service.

What we collect, and why

DataWhy we collect itLawful basis
Your CV and the job description you provideTo generate your personalised interview prep reportPerformance of a contract (you've paid for this specific output)
Your recorded voice answers and their transcriptsTo draft STAR-structured answers from your own wordsConsent (you actively choose to record) and performance of a contract
Name, email, and (if applicable) company name for a coaching callTo book and run your 20-minute coaching add-onPerformance of a contract
Payment detailsTo take payment for the report and/or add-onPerformance of a contract — handled entirely by our payment processor (Stripe); we never see or store your card details

How long we keep it

  • CVs, job descriptions and uploaded files: processed to generate your report, then deleted from our servers immediately — not retained afterwards.
  • Voice recordings: deleted as soon as they've been transcribed to text.
  • Transcripts and generated report content: not stored after your report is delivered to you, unless you explicitly ask us to keep a copy for support purposes.
  • Coaching booking details (name, email, company, chosen time): kept until the call has taken place, then deleted within 30 days, unless you've asked us to keep in touch.

Who we share it with

We use third-party processors to deliver the Service. They only ever process your data to provide their specific function, under their own data protection agreements — they don't use it for their own purposes.

  • Anthropic — processes your CV, job description and transcript to generate report content.
  • OpenAI — processes your recorded audio to produce a text transcript.
  • Stripe — processes payment information. We never see or store full card details.
  • Render (our hosting provider) — stores data only for as long as it takes to process your request, per the retention rules above.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to: access the data we hold about you; ask us to correct or delete it; object to or restrict certain processing; and complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you think we've got something wrong. To exercise any of these, email neil.copping@thecommonpeople.co.uk — we'll respond within 30 days.

Security

All data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS). Uploaded files and recordings are processed and deleted automatically rather than left sitting on disk. Access to any data we do retain (such as coaching bookings) is limited to what's needed to run the Service.

Cookies

This Service does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies.

Changes to this notice

We'll update this page if what we collect or how we use it changes, and update the date at the top accordingly.
