Answering with STAR

For each question below, structure your answer as: Situation (set the scene briefly), Task (what you were responsible for), Action (what YOU actually did — say "I," not "we"), Result (what happened, with numbers if you have them). Record your answer out loud, or just type it — either way, we'll tidy it into a clean STAR structure for your report.

Recording uses your microphone and sends the audio to our transcription provider to convert it to text — see our privacy notice for details. Typing instead skips this entirely.
